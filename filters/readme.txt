These files can be passed to parsing scripts to limit the json files parsed by the script
filterForty.txt, filters the samples not belonging to the 60k family
filterLabelsClean.txt, filters all the samples that have labels and do no belong to the 60k family
filterLabels.txt, filters all the samples that have labels
filterSixty.txt, filters all the samples belonging to the 60k family
filter.txt, filters the 23k dataset
filterUnlabelled.txt, filters all the samples that do not have labels
